Performance Record of J.S. Bach – Invention No. 10 in G Major, BWV 781

Overview: A performance record of Bach's Invention No. 10 (BWV 781), recorded in February 2025 by Reiji at age 8. This document includes the performer's unique insights into harmonic consistency and overtone resonance, revealing a structural clarity rarely observed in other Inventions.

Reiji's Observations

  • This piece is unusual in that almost every measure fits perfectly within a chord (whereas, typically, there is at least one note outside the chord, but in this piece, such notes are almost nonexistent).
  • Therefore, I interpreted each measure as a harmonic unit and focused on resonance while playing.

* These are personal reflections by the performer.
